How It Works

Built for the person standing up to teach.

Grounded

Scripture first, KJV/NKJV anchored

Every study opens from a real question, not a proof-text. Claims are checked against the Greek or Hebrew where it changes the meaning, and every interpretive point traces back to the text or a named, reputable source — never invented, never guessed.

Teachable

Ready to hand to a group

Context, walkthrough, cross-references, application, and discussion questions a leader can use as-is — not a devotional to read alone, but material built to survive being taught out loud.

AI-Assisted

Research and video, done right

AI helps pull research and draft short video companions for each topic — symbolic, non-literal visuals only, never a depiction of God, Christ, or biblical figures. The study is always the source; the video is a distillation of it.
